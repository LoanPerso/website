# Security and compliance

> Last updated: February 10, 2026

Quickfund OÜ operates with a responsible lending model: protect personal data, verify identity, prevent fraud, and comply with applicable European rules.

## 1. Regulatory framework

- Entity: Quickfund OÜ (Estonia)
- Supervisor: Finantsinspektsioon
- Main rules: GDPR, KYC/AML, Consumer Credit Directive, ePrivacy
- Under ongoing assessment: DORA, NIS2

## 2. Data protection

### Processed data

- Identity and contact data
- Financial data required for application assessment
- Technical data (security logs, IP address)

### Applied principles

- Data minimization: only data needed for the service
- Purpose limitation: processing stays within declared uses
- Retention control: deletion or archiving based on legal duties

### Security controls

- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Restricted access control
- Logging and monitoring for sensitive access
- Regular technical backups

## 3. Identity verification and anti-fraud

- Document verification and automated checks
- Manual review for sensitive files
- Blocking of clear fraud attempts
- Reporting to authorities when legally required

## 4. Responsible lending

- Solvency assessment before any credit decision
- Clear refusal rationale when a file is not suitable
- Transparent disclosure of total credit cost
- Client support in case of repayment difficulties

## 5. Your rights

Under GDPR, you can exercise your rights (access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, portability) by contacting:

- legal@quickfund.ee
- legal@quickfund.fr

## 6. Continuous improvement

Our security and compliance framework is continuously improved based on:

- incident feedback
- regulatory updates
- internal and external audits
